KING KONG

SummerStage Theater
East River Park, Manhattan, NY Aug 21(Sat) - Aug 23 (Fri) 08:00 pm - 09:30 pm
More Info

Set in the "Bronx is Burning" era of the late 1970s, SummerStage's "King Kong" celebrates the nascent forms of hop-hop music and graffiti art that grew out of the economically stressed South Bronx and went on to become one of the defining, most powerful and profitable artistic movements in the world
