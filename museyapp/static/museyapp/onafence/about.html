What we do

The team at MUSEY thinks non-stop about how to help artists extract value from the awesome, creative content they put into the world.

MUSEY is a website and mobile app that connects audiences and artists. It allows appreciative audiences to tip artists in real-time. Our mission is to help everyone see the value of the art and events all around them.

We’d love for you to become an early member of the MUSEY community. To speak with a member of our team, contact us at hello@museyapp.com
